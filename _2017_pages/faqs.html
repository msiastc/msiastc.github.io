Below is a list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ).

  1. What is MSTC all about and what can I gain from the event?
    MSTC is a conference that provides a platform for participants to engage and obtain insight upon challenges in the STEM industry, by its’ members from various backgrounds within the field. We believe that the exchange in ideas in between participants is the opportunity for everyone to gain understanding regarding modern technologies implemented within our societies and in hopes with that, we will be able to effectively improve the quality of life for others.

  2. Where will MSTC be held?
    This year’s MSTC will be held at UEM Group Berhad. The full address for the venue will be as: 17-2 Mercu UEM, Jalan Stesen Sentral 5 Kuala Lumpur Sentral, Wilayah Persekutuan, 50470 Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

  3. Who is/are the host(s) for MSTC?
    MSTC is the trademark event for YME, Young Malaysian Engineers. YME, which is a Malaysian students-run society based in the United Kingdom, serves to empower young, inexperienced engineers to develop within the STEM industry by establishing opportunities for the engagement between engineers and engineers-to-be.

  4. If the event is mainly focused towards engineers and engineers-to-be, is it also open to other Malaysian students/graduates from any other careers?
    We strongly encourage all Malaysian students studying within colleges and universities from all around the world to participate within the event, as it is open for all members within the Malaysian society to learn about the interesting STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) topics and discover various career choices based on what was gained within the event itself!

  5. Who will be presenting within the event?
    Our line-up of speakers features unique individuals from engineers to entrepreneurs with prominent contributions within the STEM field. Click on the link(hyperlink) to get accessed on more information regarding our speakers!

  6. How do I get to the venue?
    It is encouraged for participants to take public transport towards the location as it is close (a 6 minutes’ walk) to KL Sentral, Kuala Lumpur’s main integrated rail transportation centre. If driving is more to your convenience, the exact Google Map location can be found: here.

  7. How long is the duration of the event and what format will the conference be on?
    The event will be held for one and half day and is divided into 3 sections. The first day of the event consists of 2 sections while the last section will be carried out on the second day. In between sessions on the first day, a corporate luncheon will be held.

  8. When does the registration start for that day?
    Registrations will start at 8:30am, on the 12nd of August 2017. Remember to present a copy of your welcome invitational email in physical form/mobile on the day itself as verification!.

  14. What is the dress-code for the conference?
    Formal/Smart-casual. It is advised for participants that are participating in the corporate luncheon to attend in formal attire.

  15. Can I take photos or record within the event?
    Flash photography and recording during presentations or panel sessions are strictly prohibited since it will interrupt the operations of the video crew present within the event. It is also recommended that you do not ask for the video crew’s assistance in taking photos or videos during breaks within the event.

  17. Is there any parking lots around the venue if I were to drive there?
    Because Mercu UEM is located near KL Sentral, participants are highly encouraged to opt for public transportations. For participants with their own transportations, parking is not allowed within the building. However, there are parking spaces at NU Sentral or Plaza Sentral available at RM 4 flat rate per day on weekends
